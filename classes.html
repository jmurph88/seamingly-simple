Classes

Beginner

This class is for the beginner. These students have little to no sewing knowlegde. Sewing basics, such as parts of the machine, how to stitch a straight line, and proper cutting and pinning tachniques will be taught during this class.

Intermediate

This class is for sewist who know how to operate a sewing machine and sew basic stitches. More complex tasks, such as, darts, zippers, and more complicated stitches will be taught during this class.

Open Sew

Open sew is time when the sewing studio is open to the public. An instructor will be present to assist with your personal projects. Sewists of all ability levels are invited to attend!
